    April 20th: Friend of a Farmer

    If you want to feel at home the next time you dine, make sure to check out this homely restaurant.

    When living in Brooklyn Heights freshman year, there were many restaurant options on Montague St. However, one that definitely catches your eye is Friend of A Farmer with it's sheek, homely, outdoorsy setup. Inside, you'll find a fireplace in a living room-designed area with an upstairs balcony for dining. It's a great place to go to when they sun's out and you're ready to eat.
