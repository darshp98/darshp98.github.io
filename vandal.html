    March 17th: Vandal

    If you take two steps outside the 2nd St NYU Residence Hall, you'll find this restaurant 2 feet away from you.

    I think out of all things I've eaten in the city, this place had the craziest one to offer: squid ink pasta. I know this sounds alarming, and trust me, it looked alarming. However, it was one of the tastiest meals I've ever had and gave me a unique experience.

    Try it out and see what fun preparation they have in store for you!
