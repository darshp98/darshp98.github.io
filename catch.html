    April 17th: Catch

    Located near the Highline, this place serves amazing aesthetics along with amazing dishes.

    I went to this restaurant for a Saturday brunch with friends and instantly fell in love. This restaurant is not only located in a gorgeous area with a waterfront view, but also has some eye-catching meals as well. If you go, make sure to try out their shrimp tempura!
