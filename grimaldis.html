    April 15th: Grimaldi's

    If you take two steps outside the 2nd St NYU Residence Hall, you'll find this restaurant 2 feet away from you.

    The restaurant, Grimaldi's, reminds me of all my freshman year experiences I had in Brooklyn. Located near DUMBO, Grimaldi's is where you need to go to get your classi NY pizza to take with you to the Brooklyn Pier. Not only will you get a great bite, but also a great view.

    Take a bite and enjoy the DUMBO sights!
