    April 5th: Phebes

    Whether you want to have a chill Sunday brunch, or you want to watch the football game, your go-to should be Phebe's.

    This place has it all, and for cheap prices (compared to most other brunch places in NYC). This place is only two blocks away from me, so I go very often when I want to eat something that isn't from an NYU dining hall. Make sure to go for not only the food, but also for the bar nights they have!
